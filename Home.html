About

All our programs are delivered through a blended learning approach that combines market-aligned courses, a classroom team of talented mentors with the skills and knowledge to deliver growth and results for learners, and an environment that supports students creativity,job market preparation in a fun, open,and transformative learning experience. We believe that quality education should be accessible to all who want to learn regardless of their financial circumstances .

Your Moringa education will now be made affordable and accessible through a number of students Financing options such as Loans and Partial Scholarships. Our Hybrid model combines in-person instruction with online or virtual components,offering flexible and blended approach to education.

What is the mission of the Moringa School

Moringa School's mission is twofold;To empower people & To transform education through rapid-iteration.The vision for our software development product is to transform the talent landscape in Africa with world-class software engineers and software-savvy professionals.
